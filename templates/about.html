Roller Ball Simulation

Made by Jake Gendreau and Tavin Yorgason for Crimson Code Hackathon 2025!


Uses gyro sensor to control the direction of the ball

Uses microphone sound level to change the size of the ball

Uses your camera to determine the color of the ball (no iOS, because APPLE despises developers >:( )

Slider options are available in the menu!

You can tap to teleport the ball.
